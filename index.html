Haskell Weekly

Haskell Weekly is a free email newsletter about the Haskell programming language. Each issue features several hand-picked links to interesting content about Haskell from around the web.

Subscribe now to get a newsletter delivered to your inbox every Thursday. It's easy to unsubscribe and we'll never send you spam. If you'd prefer, you can follow us on Twitter or subscribe to the feed.
